Background

What

Technology is not defined as a electric device or software but refers to innovation and development of new tools, techniques, and systems that bring value to human life. Value is defined by the happiness that is brought upon a person and technology has been the strongest driver of this postive change.

How

A few examples of advancements though not all:

  • Rise of Social Media spreading information around the world like wildfire
  • 20% of the US Workforce works remotely
  • Rise of Streaming Services changing the way people consume content
  • Wearable technology fundamentally changing personal tracking
  • Development of Artificial Intelligence which has shocked the globe for its reasoning, effiency, and capabilities
    • It is important to note that these advancements are not just related to technology, but has rebuilt virutally every industry.
