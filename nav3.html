What is next?

Why

  • Technology must constantly improve and grow to maintain its impact or else it will lose its value
  • Constant development requires a skilled workforce to drive innovation and maintain value
  • For example, artificial intelligence must rapidly evolve to stay relevant and effective for humanity

    • What is next

  • After Generative AI, the next form of AI is Agentic AI capable of executing complex tasks autonomously

    • For example, a agent could completely plan the next trip from planning, booking, and managing the entire vacation

  • After Agentic AI, the next phase is AGI which is AI exceeding human capabilites across cognitive tasks, reasoning, and problem solving
  • After AGI, the next phase is Quantam Computing which will be using Qubits - rather 1 or 0s, it can do both simultaneously

    • Other advancements in the horizon are

  • SpaceX going to Mars
  • Robotics and Electric Vehicles helping solve climate change
  • Stronger understanding of human evolutionary genetics to understand how humanity has developed over time
