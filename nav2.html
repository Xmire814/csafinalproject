Caveats

It is important to understand the negative impacts of technology

Social Media Dangers

  • Platforms creating repeated dopamine hits can make them extremely addictive
  • Social Media has lead to higher rates of anxiety, depression, and mental health issues
  • Increased screen time can be detrimental to eye health, physical activity, and sleep quality

    • Artificial Intelligence Waste

  • AI Systems require significant computational power which leads to high energy usage
  • Even more, AI servers require large amounts of water for cooling, a midsized data center consumes as much water as a small town
  • Overall, AI development has a huge carbon footprint and significant environmental consequences

    • Cybersecurity Significance

  • As the world grows more interconnected, so does the reliance on digital systems which can store important data
  • Cyber attacks can compromise vital information and be devastating to millions of people
  • With growing technology, the need for robust cybersecurity measures is more important than before
